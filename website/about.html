👻 What is SpecterOS?

SpecterOS is a privacy-first, Debian-based Linux distribution designed for security researchers, privacy advocates, journalists, activists, and anyone who values their digital freedom.

In an era of increasing surveillance, data breaches, and privacy erosion, SpecterOS provides a secure foundation for your digital life. Built on the solid foundation of Debian 12, enhanced with cutting-edge privacy technologies and security features.

🎯 Our Mission

We believe privacy is a fundamental human right. Our mission is to make privacy-focused computing accessible to everyone, not just security experts.

SpecterOS combines enterprise-grade security with user-friendly design, making it easy for anyone to take control of their digital privacy.
